Frequently asked questions

What does the Closely–Pipedrive integration do?
It automatically syncs campaign data from Closely—such as LinkedIn messages, email blasts, and engagement metrics—into Pipedrive, creating real-time records for every interaction.
Which campaign data gets synced over to Pipedrive?
All outgoing and incoming messages, email interactions including opens, as well as connection acceptance events—captured with actual send/receive timestamps, not sync timestamps.
Can you configure what kind of contacts sync to Pipedrive?
es. During campaign setup, choose whether to sync all contacts, only those who accepted connection requests, or only those who replied—ensuring clean and focused lead data flows into Pipedrive.
How are campaign performance metrics handled?
Metrics like response rates, connection acceptance rates, email deliverability, opens, and A/B test results sync into Pipedrive. You can slice data by campaign channel, persona, or variant to refine outreach strategy.
How does this support pipeline automation?
With detailed campaign analytics and synced tags (e.g. “demo”, “positive”) flowing into Pipedrive, you can automate progress through stages, follow‑ups, scoring, and funnel automation—all visible within the CRM dashboard in real time.
